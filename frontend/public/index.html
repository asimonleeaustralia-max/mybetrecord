Frequently asked questions

What is a sports betting tracker?

A structured ledger for every wager — stakes, odds, outcomes, and profit — with automatic ROI, yield, and breakdowns instead of manual spreadsheet formulas.

Which odds formats are supported?

Decimal, American, fractional, Hong Kong, Malaysian, and Indonesian. All bets are stored with canonical decimal odds for reporting.

How does Kelly stake work here?

Enter personal implied odds on a bet. With bankroll and Kelly multiplier set in settings, the app recommends a stake based on your stated edge.

Can I export or use an API?

Yes — CSV, Excel, and JSON exports plus API keys for programmatic access to your record.

Is this a tipster service?

No. mybetrecord is record-keeping and analytics only. We do not sell picks or promise profits.
